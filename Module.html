Amenities

  
  

We at Mahalaxmi Infra always focus on providing the best combination of amenities for projects at different locations in all our layouts. All our developments in the projects are done as per the rules and regulations of the authorities like NMRDA.

Internal Cement Concrete Road

A Paved road within the premises connecting all major parts

Garden

Landscaped green space with plantation for better experience.

Sewage Treatment Plant

Wastewater processing system installed within the layout.

Sewage Line

Strom Water Drainage

Systematic planning to manage rainwater runoff.

Cricket Pitch

Electric Network With Transformer

Open Space Public Utility

Kids Park

Basket Ball

Basketball court for outdoor sports.

Meditation Centre

Dedicated Space for contemplation and relaxation.
